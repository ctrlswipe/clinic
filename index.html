The Hidden Crisis in Dutch Healthcare

While the Netherlands has a robust healthcare system, the gap for specialized geriatric physiotherapy is widening. Many seniors suffer in silence due to the high costs of supplementary insurance. ZilverRug Kliniek bridges this gap with state-subsidized programs (*Zorgtoeslag supported).

"Pain should not be the price of aging. We ensure every senior in Amsterdam has access to world-class spinal care, regardless of their pension."
